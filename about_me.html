About me

The theme I chose is based on my article on One Piece because all my other articles coincide with it. In One Piece, there is a denunciation of pollution which I did with my article on my BU book. With the place, the hopes and regrets, join One Piece, because there is racism, malnutrition and some people who help the most precarious. And finally, my music coincides with my general theme of the current world with money at the centre of interests, mass consumption, the hazards of renewal, people addicted to their phones, ...

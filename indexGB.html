Denunciation of the current world

Here are the different articles which all coincide on one and the same theme, the distresses of the present world. We will use different types of content on this theme. There will be an article against a music, a book, a place, a series, a regret and finally a hope.

An interesting music


In this article, you will find the description of the lyrics of Hakai's music Satellite.

Content of the article: With these lyrics, the artist shows us that the world is obnibulated by money and consumption. Perhaps we can even talk about mass consumption.



The book for the environment


In this article, you will find my description of the book made by APPA, its title is "Air pollution".

Content of the article: We will see what APPA tells us about air pollution. But as the book dates from 1998, we will see the trends from that time and compare them with the current trends.



The Series that sums it all up


In this article, you will find my description of the One Piece series created by Eiichirō Oda.

Content of the article: We'll see what Oda is up to in his work. He makes a lot of hidden allusions which makes his style intriguing and interesting.



Most unequal place in the world


In this article, you will find my description of the place. The chosen place is South Africa.

Content of the article: We will see why we chose this place. South Africa is the most insecure country in the world.



A great hope for the world


In this article you will find my description of a hope of my generation. My hope is the people who help the most disadvantaged.

Content of the article: We are going to enumerate a lot of systems to help the poor. In particular with the associations or even the kitty.



A regret of this generation


In this article, you will find my description of a regret of my generation. My regret is on the theme of inequality in the world of food.

Content of the article: We will list a lot of inequalities in the world. With the poor access to water and food in the world.

What it means to make music

With these words, the artist shows us that the world is omnibulated by money and consumption, perhaps we can speak of mass consumption. There is an imprisonment of the person on his phone with the notifications and it is also a working tool because these notifications also show his progress in his business, the people call him only for the business and thus to earn money which shows the mass consumption. So there is a little bit of passion in his music but his main goal is to earn as much money as possible and he makes music more to consume and earn a lot. People envy him with the notifications he gets because it's the money that comes to him in a way. These notifications are all about the business, people only care about the money and not about him. It shows that people are tired of working to earn their money on a daily basis. It shows that people kind of like to hurt themselves hence the word 'maso' which means masochist. It shows that he starts working when he knows how much he will earn before he starts.

Image of the artist Hakai in a dark red and black atmosphere

It shows us that the trend is only changing and very quickly. It can change overnight. One day you'll have the glory and overnight it can all fall apart. He means that when you are at your lowest point nobody will be there to help you, either mentally or even physically. He does not work to consume products but rather to travel to heavenly places. According to him, traffickers who make money behind the backs of their "friends" are really despicable mentally because you may think that this person can be close to you but it is not the case. This shows that he has lived with a traitor who has shown us previously and that he is tired of being betrayed by people close to him and since he is vulgar this accentuates his displeasure with this type of action which he hates. Then he wants the traitors to take responsibility and stop making excuses. He talks to a traitor, he tries to intimidate him. He tells him why you act like you didn't do anything when you are the guilty one. And on top of that he comes back as if nothing had happened as if he was still his friend. He doesn't know yet if he'll get any profit out of it but he's trying to make his parents feel good so they can afford whatever they want.

To conclude on music

We can see that the world is omnibulated by money. Each person always wants more than what they already have, whether it is to consume or to earn money. We have also seen that the telephone is a focus for almost everyone. People become addicted to their phones and use them as working tools. We can also see that people like to do harm to themselves, here getting up for a job they don't like. We also see a lot of people who change completely over time. But the main theme of the music is money and somehow the world is controlled by money. The world is all about money and if you don't have enough it's hard to live, whether it's food or even indulging in consumption so it's almost impossible to survive if you don't have any.
